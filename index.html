The Cyber Security Club at Virginia Tech is a student organization in Computer Engineering and Computer Science at Virginia Tech founded in spring 2011 with the goal of encouraging and expanding the study of cyber security and related fields. We aim to develop awareness and expertise in all areas of cyber security including physical, social, hardware, and software.
